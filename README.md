# 🏥 Patient No-Show Appointments Analysis

Overview
This project analyzes a healthcare appointments dataset to identify patterns in patient no-shows. Understanding why patients miss appointments is critical for healthcare providers to optimize scheduling, reduce revenue loss, and improve patient care.

Problem Statement
Patient no-shows are a major operational challenge in healthcare. This analysis explores key factors — such as age, gender, day of week, SMS reminders, and chronic conditions — that influence whether a patient shows up for their appointment.

Dataset

Source: Public healthcare appointments dataset
Records: 110,000+ appointment entries
Key Fields: PatientID, ScheduledDay, AppointmentDay, Age, Gender, No-show status, SMS received

Tools & Libraries Used

Python
Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib
Seaborn

Key Analysis Performed

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Age group segmentation
No-show rate by gender, age, and day of week
Impact of SMS reminders on attendance
Visualization of scheduling patterns

Key Findings

Younger patients showed higher no-show rates
SMS reminders had a measurable impact on attendance
Certain days of the week showed consistently higher no-show rates
